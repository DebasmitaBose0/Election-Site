Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and privacy

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of May 5, 2026.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information when you take the voter pledge
  • Location data for finding polling stations and representatives
  • Chat messages sent to our AI assistant

Technical Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Device information for accessibility features

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide election information and services
  • To generate voter pledge certificates
  • To improve our AI chat responses
  • To ensure platform security and prevent abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who help operate our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure data storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Input sanitization to prevent injection attacks

Data Retention

  • Voter pledge data is retained for 7 years for verification purposes
  • Chat logs are anonymized after 30 days
  • Technical logs are retained for 90 days
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers (Google Gemini AI, Google Civic API) may process data outside India. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and include appropriate safeguards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing in certain circumstances
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@electionant.gov.in

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences (font size, high contrast mode)
  • Provide secure authentication
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our service

You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with:

  • Google Gemini AI: For chat assistance
  • Google Civic Information API: For representative lookup
  • Google Maps: For location services
  • GNews API: For election news

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs how they handle data.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through our platform or via email. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@electionant.gov.in
  • Phone: 1800-XXX-XXXX (toll-free)
  • Address: Election Commission of India, New Delhi
